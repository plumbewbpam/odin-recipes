*✧･ﾟ:* taylor's chai sugar cookies *:･ﾟ✧*

this recipe is for swifties and dessert lovers,

enjoy these yummy cookies, perfect for the fall season!

ingredients for cookies:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
  • pinch fresh cracked black pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • cinnamon sugar for rolling

ingredients for glaze

  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons whole milk (or eggnog)

instructions:

  1. preheat the oven to 350F. line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. using a mixer, beat the butter in a large bowl on medium speed for about 1 minute. add the vegetable oil, granulated sugar, powdered sugar, and all of the spices. beat to combine.
  3. add the egg and vanilla, beating on medium speed until completely mixed.
  4. stir in flour, baking soda, and salt with mixer set on low.
  5. the dough will be soft. refrigerate for 1 hour or freeze for 15 minutes.
  6. use 1 tablespoon of cookie dough onto the cookie sheet. place them about 2 inches apart. roll each cookie dough in a small bowl of cinnamon sugar.
  7. bake for 8 to 10 minutes. let cookies cool on baking sheet for 10 minutes, transfer to cooling rack for additional 5-10 minutes.
  8. in a medium bowl, whisk together glaze ingredients til thick but spreadable. put on cookies.
  9. store cookies in airtight container at room temp for up to 4 days.
  10. enjoy!
