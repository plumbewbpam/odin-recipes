*✧･ﾟ:* easiest chocolate chip cookies *:･ﾟ✧*

this recipe is the easiest chocolate chip cookie recipe,

with basic ingredients, no mixer, and only 20 minutes...

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar [packed]
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 350F.
  2. microwave butter for 40 sesconds. should be melted, but not hot.
  3. mix butter with sugars until well-combined in a large bowl.
  4. stir in vanilla and egg until incorporated.
  5. add the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. mix dough until just combined. soft and a little sticky.
  7. stir in chocolate chips.
  8. scoop out 1.5 tablespoons of dough per cookie.
  9. place 2 inches apart on baking sheet.
  10. bake 7-10 minutes, or until set.
