*✧･ﾟ:* classic pb cookies *:･ﾟ✧*

this recipe is grandma's iconic quick dessert,

the perfect dessert for fireplaces and glasses of milk...

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar [lightly packed]
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder

instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 350F.
  2. sift flour and baking powder together, whisk to combine.
  3. cream butter and sugars in stand mixer.
  4. add peanut butter and mix until incorporated.
  5. mix in egg and vanilla, add flour mixture and beat until incorporated.
  6. roll dough into 1 inch balls, place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. flatten cookies with a fork in criss-cross pattern.
  8. bake cookies for about 10 minutes.
  9. allow cookies to cool completely on baking sheet before transferring.
